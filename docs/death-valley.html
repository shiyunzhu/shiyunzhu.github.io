Death Valley

Is that snow on the hottest place on Earth? Visit Badwater Basin to find out for yourself. The otherwordly landscapes you'll encounter will include desert sand dunes and scenes straight out of Star Wars. Unsurprisingly the lowest and driest place in the country is not very populated, making it an ideal dark sky to look for the aliens commonly thought to reside in nearby Area 51. Look for UFOs amongst the Ursids in tonight's meteor shower.
